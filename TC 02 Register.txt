*** Ornament tattoo studio ***
Register page http://ornament.esy.es/account/Register
Description: The purpose of this story is to test functionalities of the Register page of Ornament tattoo studio site
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Click on "Your account" button and wait the "Login" page to open.
"Login" page is displayed properly.

Click on "Register" button
"Register" page is displayed.

Leave the fields empty and click "Register" button.
Appropriate error messages are displayed (for all fields).

Enter invalid email address into "Email" input field and click on "Register" button.
Error messages are properly displayed for:
	- "Email" field
	- "User name" field
	- "Password" field
	- "Repeat Password" field

Enter valid email address into "Email" input field and click on "Register" button.
Error messages are properly displayed for:
	- "User name" field
	- "Password" field
	- "Repeat Password" field

Enter 5 or less characters into "User name" input field and click on "Register" button.
Error messages are properly displayed for:
	- "User name" field
	- "Password" field
	- "Repeat Password" field

Enter 6 or more characters into "User name" input field and click on "Register" button.
Error messages are properly displayed for:
	- "Password" field
	- "Repeat Password" field

Enter 6 or less characters into "Password" input field and click on "Register" button.
Error messages are properly displayed for:
	- "Password" field
	- "Repeat Password" field

Enter 7 or more characters into "Password" input field and click on "Register" button.
Error messages are properly displayed for:
	- "Repeat Password" field

Enter different password into "Repeat Password" input field than is used in previous step and click on "Register" button.
Error messages are properly displayed for:
	- "Repeat Password" field

Enter valid data for all fields and click on "Register" button.
"Login" page is successfully displayed.
